Frequently Asked Questions

How does the contract-based system work?

You submit an online contract via our website. After submitting, the contract will have a status of Pending. After the product is delivered to you, the contract will have a status of Delivered. The contract is renewed on a yearly basis for free. The renewal is for inspections and maintenance purposes. There is also a baseline 1-year warranty.

Can I opt out of inspections?

You can opt out of inspections for most products, except fire extingushers, fire hoses, and sprinklers. Fo the aformentioned products, inspections are mandatory, thus opting out of them requires cancelling/not renewing the product, which means your product will not be supported by Monoxxal Safety Systems.

How much are repair/replacement costs?

Repair/replacement costs typically range from Kshs.250.00 to Kshs.8,500.00 per product, depending on the product type and level of disrepair.
